My top 3 movies of all time

What's wrong with secetary Kim

This is one of the best korean movies of all time that has a setting in the corporate world between a boss and his secretary who had met when they were young but due to some incidents at the point of their meeting these years back, they got entangled in lies, and family problems. but they came out triumphant at the end of the day.

Descendants of the Sun

This is a trago-comedy movie set in a hospital and an army location for special forces. A love story between a doctor and an army captain Yoo.

Oh My Venus

This is also a Korean love story between a lawyer, former beauty queen and a doctor/heir to a conglomerate company who try to find things to get what they want.
